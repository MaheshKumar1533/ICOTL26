About The College

Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science (MITS), established in 1998 under the Ratakonda Ranga Reddy Educational Academy, is a premier institution nestled in the scenic surroundings of Madanapalle on a 26.17-acre campus along NH-205. Founded under the leadership of Late Sri N. Krishna Kumar, M.S. (U.S.A.), and visionary Chancellor Dr. N. Vijaya Bhaskar Choudary, MITS has achieved 27 years of academic excellence and has been conferred the status of a Deemed to be University under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956. About The Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was established in the year 1998. The Department has obtained UGC-Autonomous Status in the year 2014 and is running the UG & PG programmes successfully meeting all the requirements. The programme ensures that the student effectively meets the highest benchmarks of competence required by the industry. Students' forums such as ISTE, IEEE, IEI & CSI were formed mainly to encourage and motivate students.

About The Conference

The 2nd International Conference on Optimization Techniques for Learning (ICOTL 2026) is a major international conference to bring together researchers, engineers, and scientists from academia and industry working in Machine Learning, Data Science, other relevant domains and their applications in different scientific, technical and social optimization processes. Special sessions on various subjects related to artificial intelligence, soft-computing, intelligent systems, cognitive science, data analysis, IoT are welcome.
